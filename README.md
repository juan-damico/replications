## About
This repository documents the full empirical workflow of applied economic and econometric work: interpretation of academic research, reconstruction of datasets from public sources, implementation of econometric models, and presentation of results. It offers a transparent and reproducible record of technical and analytical capabilities, and may be of interest for research, collaboration, or professional evaluation.

All replications are built independently from scratch. Datasets are reconstructed from public databases (FRED, World Bank) following the variable definitions in each paper. All code is written by Juan D'Amico; minor discrepancies with original results may arise due to differences in data vintages or software.
